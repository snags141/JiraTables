General Controls

Table Width

Specify how wide you want your tables to display in percentages. For example: 100 is default, 300 might be more optimal for tables with a larger amount of content - Play around with different values until you find something that works for you.

Units selector

Currently the extension only sets values in Percentages, but in future will support exact pixels as a unit.
